# IDEAL CANDIDATE PROFILE (ICP) – LEVEL 1
# ROLE: AI DEVELOPER

## 1. BASIC ROLE DETAILS

- Role Name: AI Developer / AI Automation Developer
- Department: AI, Technology & Automation
- Reporting To: AI Manager / Founder / Tech Head
- Experience Required: 1–3 years of practical experience in AI tools, automation, APIs, or internal software development
- Salary Range: ₹25,000 – ₹45,000 per month, depending on skill, portfolio, and problem-solving ability
- Location: Mumbai, Khar West / Office-based
- Work Timing: 11:00 AM – 8:00 PM

---

# 2. MAIN PURPOSE OF THIS ROLE

To build, improve, and maintain AI-powered tools, automations, internal systems, and workflows that help the company save time, improve team productivity, reduce manual work, and make business processes faster and more accurate.

---

# 3. WHAT KIND OF PERSON ARE WE LOOKING FOR?

The ideal candidate should be:

- Stable and looking for long-term work
- Open to learning new AI tools, APIs, and automation systems
- Responsible and accountable for delivery
- Hungry to grow in AI, automation, and business technology
- Disciplined and punctual with timelines
- Positive in attitude and solution-oriented
- Comfortable with feedback and repeated improvements
- Able to work in a fast-paced business environment
- Emotionally mature when systems break or urgent fixes are needed
- Respectful with team members from non-technical departments
- Able to explain technical things in simple language
- Curious about improving existing processes
- Comfortable working with trial, error, debugging, and testing
- Focused on practical execution, not only theory

---

# 4. TOP 3 MOST IMPORTANT TRAITS FOR THIS ROLE

## Trait 1: Problem-Solving Mindset
Why is it important?

AI and automation work is not only about writing code. The person must understand the real business problem, identify where time is being wasted, and create a practical solution. Many times, the requirement will not be perfectly clear, so the candidate should be able to ask the right questions, test different approaches, and find a workable solution.

## Trait 2: Fast Learning Ability
Why is it important?

AI tools, APIs, automation platforms, and development methods keep changing very quickly. The candidate should not wait for spoon-feeding. They should be able to learn from documentation, tutorials, previous code, and testing. A fast learner will help the company adopt new tools before competitors.

## Trait 3: Ownership and Reliability
Why is it important?

Internal systems like CRM, lead automation, Google Sheets, WhatsApp automation, dashboards, and AI tools directly affect daily operations. If something breaks, the business can slow down. The candidate must take ownership, test properly, give updates, and not leave work half-done.

# 5. STABILITY CHECK (VERY IMPORTANT)

We prefer candidates who:

- Want long-term growth in AI, automation, and technology
- Can stay minimum 2+ years
- Have stable job history or strong project consistency
- Stay close to workplace or can travel reliably
- Are looking for career growth, not only quick salary jumps
- Have genuine interest in building business systems
- Are willing to work on internal company projects, not only trendy AI experiments
- Can handle both new development and maintenance work

### HR Should Check:

- Frequent job changes
- Large unexplained gaps
- Pattern of leaving quickly after learning basics
- Too many career switches without clear reason
- Complaining about previous companies or managers
- Only interested in remote work if role requires office presence
- Overclaiming skills without real projects
- Copy-paste developer mindset without understanding logic
- Lack of patience for debugging and testing
- Whether the candidate has actually completed projects end-to-end

- Lack of responsibility

- Arrogance without real project achievement
- Negative attitude toward previous employer

- Looking only for salary, not growth

### Things To Investigate Further:

- Multiple side hustles

- Unclear career direction
